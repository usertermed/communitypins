    How to Use

    1. Click on the map to add a pin with a note.

    2. Search for a location to view or pin it.

    3. Click the heart or dislike icons on pins to like or dislike them.

    4. Use the Center button to return to your location.

    About Community Pins

    Community Pins lets you mark and share locations with notes and likes. Built for the 2025 TSA Website Design competition.

    Firebase for data storage, Leaftlet for map data, ipapi.co for IP-based geolocation data.
